About us

Quattro Rioni is a local association created in 2024. It was founded with the idea of improving community life in our town. Our group organizes recreational, cultural, and charity events throughout the year, always involving citizens of all ages. Our main goal is to bring new energy to the area, strengthen the sense of community, and support the local activities and traditions of Cilavegna.

Pink asparagus festival

The Pink Asparagus of Cilavegna is a local excellence and proudly carries the De.Co label. Every year, its festival takes place on the second weekend of May and has become one of the most important events in town. During these days, visitors can enjoy historical parades with people dressed in traditional costumes, a lively street market, and many food stands celebrating this unique product.

Charity dinners

Charity dinners are an important tradition in Cilavegna, especially during the Christmas season. These events are organized to raise funds for other local non-profit associations and often take place in collaboration with the municipal administration.

Sports events

The town hosts a variety of sports events designed for people of all ages. These activities include charity runs, inclusive sports sessions, and recreational challenges that encourage everyone to take part.
